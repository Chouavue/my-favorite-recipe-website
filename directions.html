Let's get started!

  1. Boil one pot of hot water.
  2. Place chuck roast into boiling water.
  3. Cut onion and ginger into slices and throw into pot.
  4. Add salt to taste.
  5. Let simmer for 1-2 hours.
  6. Remove rice noodles from bag and soak in water until soften.
  7. Start cutting green onions and cliantro after rinsing.
  8. Take chuck roast from pot and cut into thin slices.
  9. Drain rice nooodles and add 1 serving to bowl.
  10. Add slices of meat, green onion, cliantro. You can add lime or jalenpenos if you like.
  11. Scoop beef broth from pot into bowl until noodles are covered.
  12. Add siracha and hoisin to taste.
